What We Do

Since 1953, Elevator World, Inc. has served the international building transportation industry. It employs specialists in Mobile, Alabama, and has technical and news correspondents around the world. The staff is involved in editorial, production, advertising, circulation, marketing and administrative activities related to both print, online and digital publishing. Divisions of the company are magazine publishing, including our flagship magazine ELEVATOR WORLD (EW), which is read in more than 80 countries; educational/book publishing; continuing education and certification that includes at least six accredited articles published in EW each year; three email newsletters that reach more than 30,000 industry personnel; and an online network of websites visited by over 900,000 industry professionals yearly
